## Specifications:

**Output:** 10mV / 1°C

**Range :** -1V(-55°C) to 6V(150°C)

---

**Pinout:**
- `Pin 1` **VCC** (+5V)
- `Pin 2` **AnalogOut** (Connected to ADC (Pin: PA0))
- `Pin 3` **GND** (Ground)
